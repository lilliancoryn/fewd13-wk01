About

Lillian began her career at Fabrica, Benetton's center for communications research in Italy, designing online for COLORS magazine. She then spent 10 years at digital agencies in New York and London, working with global brands in technology, fashion and finance.

In 2008, Lillian co-founded Well Made World, a studio for projects that shift the way we live in the 21st century. Clients include the United Nations, the Ad Council, and many innovative startups. In her free time, Lillian helps run SheSays, a global creative network for women that has been featured in FastCompany, NYTimes and AdWeek.
